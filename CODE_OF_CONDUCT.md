# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone. All contributors are expected to interact professionally and respectfully.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Providing constructive feedback.
* Helping others learn and grow.
* Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insulting language.
* Spam or disruptive behavior.
* Sharing confidential information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate these guidelines.

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer through the contact information provided in the repository.
